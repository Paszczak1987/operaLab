Welcome to OperaLab LIMS system.

OperaLab is an integrated laboratory data management system designed to efficiently manage laboratories, projects, personnel, and infrastructure.
Key features of the system include:

  • Laboratory information management system:
    Centralize experimental and operational data, ensuring security and easy access. Performing tests, analyzing samples, and recording results are all automated and streamlined.
  • Laboratory manager:
    Create, view, update laboratories, with full access to location, and more than essential details.
  • Personnel manager:
    View staff lists, add new employees, update information, and assign staff to laboratories. Define roles and manage permissions for staff members.
  • Projects tracker:
    Track the progress and store information of construction sites related to our laboratory facilities.

OperaLab simplifies the complex tasks of laboratory management, providing a centralized solution for efficient operation and data handling. With its intuitive interface, OperaLab centralizes data and streamlines the management of laboratory spaces, staff, and construction projects.
