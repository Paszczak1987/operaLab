Welcome to the Personnel Management application.

Personnel Manager, with its intuitive and user-friendly interface, enables efficient management of laboratory staff and their roles within the organization.
Key functionalities include:

  • View Personnel List:
    Displays a comprehensive list of all staff members, allowing users to quickly review the personnel assigned to different laboratories, including their roles and contact information.
  • Add New Staff Member:
    Easily add new employees to the system, specifying important details such as name, role (e.g., Laboratory Technician, Lab Manager, Group Leader), and the laboratory they are assigned to.
  • Staff Details and Updates:
    Effortlessly update staff information to reflect any changes in roles, assignments, or contact details, ensuring that personnel records remain accurate and up to date.
  • Delete Staff Member:
    Remove staff members who are no longer part of the organization or have moved to a different department with a simple and secure delete function.
  • Role and Permission Management:
    Define and manage roles for each staff member, providing the appropriate permissions and access levels for their responsibilities.
  • Laboratory Assignment:
    Quickly assign or reassign personnel to laboratories, supporting dynamic team structures and improving the coordination of laboratory resources.

This application is ideal for institutions or organizations that need to manage a diverse team of laboratory personnel efficiently, offering a centralized tool to maintain clear and organized records of all staff members and their responsibilities.
